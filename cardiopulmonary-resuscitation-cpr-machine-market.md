<p><p>The cardiopulmonary resuscitation (CPR) machine market encompasses two main types: mechanical CPR machines and automated CPR machines. Mechanical CPR machines provide consistent, rhythmic chest compressions to enhance blood circulation during cardiac emergencies. They are typically manually operated by trained personnel. In contrast, automated CPR machines offer advanced features like real-time monitoring and automatic adjustment of compression depth and rate, ensuring optimal performance without constant supervision. Both types are crucial in improving survival rates during critical cardiac incidents.</p></p>
