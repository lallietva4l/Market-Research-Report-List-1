<p><p>Copper CMP (Chemical Mechanical Planarization) slurries are essential for semiconductor manufacturing, used to polish copper surfaces for integrated circuits. The market is categorized into Prestonian and Non-Prestonian types. </p><p>Prestonian slurries are characterized by their response to applied pressure, where removal rate increases with pressure. In contrast, Non-Prestonian slurries exhibit a more consistent removal rate regardless of pressure variations. Each type serves different processing needs, impacting efficiency and effectiveness in achieving desired surface finishes during copper polishing.</p></p>
