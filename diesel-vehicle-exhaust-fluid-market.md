<p><p>Diesel Vehicle Exhaust Fluid (DEF) is a non-toxic solution made from high-purity urea and deionized water, used in Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR) systems to reduce nitrogen oxide emissions from diesel engines. This fluid plays a crucial role in meeting environmental regulations and improving air quality. The market for DEF is witnessing significant growth due to increasing regulatory pressure on emissions, heightened awareness of pollution, and the rising adoption of clean technologies in the automotive sector.</p><p>In recent years, the DEF market has been characterized by advancements in product formulation and distribution methods. Growth is being driven by the increasing number of diesel vehicles in commercial transportation sectors, including trucks, buses, and agricultural machinery. The expanding infrastructure for DEF supply, such as dedicated refueling stations and dispensers, is further contributing to market expansion. The Diesel Vehicle Exhaust Fluid Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.1% during the forecast period, as more manufacturers integrate SCR technology into their diesel engines, thereby increasing the demand for DEF.
