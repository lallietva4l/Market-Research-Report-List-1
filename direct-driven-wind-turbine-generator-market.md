<p><strong>The Direct-driven Wind Turbine Generator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Direct-driven Wind Turbine Generator market comprises Type I and Type II categories. Type I generators utilize a synchronous generator, directly connected to the rotor, offering high efficiency and low maintenance. These are typically favored in offshore applications. Type II generators, on the other hand, employ an induction generator with a gearbox, balancing cost and performance, and are often used in onshore projects. Both types cater to different operational needs and environmental conditions, influencing their market adoption.</p></p>
