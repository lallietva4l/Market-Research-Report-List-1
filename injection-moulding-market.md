<p><p>The injection moulding market can be categorized into three main types: electric machines, hybrid machines, and other machines. Electric machines utilize fully electric-driven systems, offering higher precision, energy efficiency, and reduced maintenance. Hybrid machines combine hydraulic and electric technologies, balancing power and energy savings. The "other" category includes traditional hydraulic machines, which are still prevalent for large-scale production due to their robust nature and lower initial costs. Each type caters to specific production needs and operational efficiencies within the industry.</p></p>
