<p><ul><li>Single-punch</li><li>Rotary</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lab scale tablet presses are crucial for pharmaceutical research and development. There are two primary types: single-punch and rotary tablet presses. Single-punch presses operate by compressing powder in a single motion, ideal for small-scale production and formulation testing. In contrast, rotary tablet presses utilize a rotating die mechanism, allowing for continuous operation and higher output, suitable for larger-scale experiments. Both types serve essential roles in optimizing tablet formulations and assessing granulation properties in a laboratory setting.</p></p>
