<p><strong>The LDI Laser Direct Imaging Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polygon Mirror 365nm</li><li>DMD 405nm</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Laser Direct Imaging (LDI) machines market encompasses technologies like polygon mirror 365nm and Digital Micromirror Device (DMD) 405nm systems. Polygon mirror 365nm LDI utilizes a rotating mirror to project laser light for high-precision imaging on photoresist materials, ideal for creating intricate patterns in PCB manufacturing. In contrast, DMD 405nm technology employs micro mirrors to dynamically control laser exposure, offering greater design flexibility and improved resolution for advanced photolithography applications. Both types cater to the evolving demands of the electronics industry.</p></p>
