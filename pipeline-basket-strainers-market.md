<p><p>Pipeline basket strainers are designed to filter out debris and particulate matter from liquids in various industrial applications. The market primarily includes two types: simplex basket strainers, which feature a single straining element and require shutdown for cleaning, making them suitable for low-flow applications; and duplex basket strainers, equipped with two straining elements that allow for continuous operation while one is being cleaned, ideal for high-flow situations where uninterrupted service is essential.</p></p>
