<p><p>The residential wall-hung boilers market primarily includes regular wall-hung boilers and condensing wall-hung boilers. Regular wall-hung boilers are traditional systems that provide hot water and heating using a conventional method, often less efficient than modern alternatives. In contrast, condensing wall-hung boilers are designed to maximize energy efficiency by utilizing exhaust gases to preheat incoming water, leading to lower energy consumption and reduced emissions. Both types are popular for their space-saving designs and effectiveness in residential heating.</p></p>
