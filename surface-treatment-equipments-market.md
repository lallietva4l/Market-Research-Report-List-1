<p><p>Surface treatment equipment market encompasses technologies that enhance surface properties for better adhesion, compatibility, and durability. Plasma treatment equipment utilizes ionized gas to modify surfaces at a molecular level, improving bonding for coatings and adhesives. In contrast, corona treatment equipment employs high-voltage electrical discharges to generate a corona discharge, which increases surface energy and promotes better receptivity for inks and adhesives. Both types play crucial roles across industries, including packaging, automotive, and electronics, optimizing performance and product longevity.</p></p>
