<p><strong>The Thermal Printers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Thermal</li><li>Thermal Transfer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermal printer market is primarily divided into two types: Direct Thermal and Thermal Transfer printers. Direct Thermal printers use heat-sensitive paper that darkens when exposed to heat, making them ideal for short-term labels such as receipts. In contrast, Thermal Transfer printers utilize a ribbon that transfers ink onto a substrate when heated, allowing for more durable and longer-lasting prints suitable for barcodes and labels needing resistance to fading. Both technologies cater to diverse printing needs across various industries.</p></p>
