<p><p>The Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS) market is primarily divided into two types: DC power supply and AC power supply. DC power supply systems convert incoming AC power to direct current, providing uninterrupted power for sensitive equipment like servers and telecommunication devices. In contrast, AC power supply systems deliver continuous alternating current, suitable for standard electrical loads and larger applications. Both types safeguard against power disruptions, ensuring reliable operation across various industries, including IT, healthcare, and manufacturing.</p></p>
